FAQ

What is Gift Search?
Gift Search is India's largest multi-category online gifting company, providing one of the best curated collection of gifts, flowers, cakes and personalized products for all personal occasions and festivals (both domestic and international). The firm has a global footprint with almost half of its orders originating from outside of India and almost one-third of its orders getting delivered internationally to more than 90 countries across the globe.
What kind of products and gifts are available at Gift Search?
Gift Search is a one-stop shop for all kinds of gifting requirement. We have created bespoke collections for almost all kinds of recipients and occasions. We handpick items from over 200 product categories. We are also market leaders in specialty product/gift categories like Personalized products, Hampers, Flowers, Cakes and Handmade chocolates. We also offer large signature collections of over 2000 products for all key festivals in India, including both domestic (Rakhi, Diwali, Bhaidooj ,etc) and international (valentine’s day, mother’s day, etc) festivals.
Where can Gift Search deliver products?
Gift Search delivers its products worldwide. We ship our products to around 20,000 pincodes in India and around 200 countries worldwide. Apart from our own delivery network, we use premium courier partners such as DHL, Blue Dart, FedEx, etc for our domestic and international deliveries. We can usually delivery domestic orders in 2-5 business days and international orders in 4-7 business days to regular pincodes. It may take up to 10-12 business days to deliver products to remote locations.
How old and big is Gift Search?
Gift Search is a 15-year old company which has reincarnated under a new professional management in last 2 years. The company recently changed its domain name from indiangiftsportal.com to igp.com. Gift Search has 2 offices and 4 warehouses with over 500 employees across Mumbai, NCR, Jaipur, Lucknow and Los Angeles.
